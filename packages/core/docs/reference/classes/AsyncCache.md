### exec()

> `static` **exec**\<`T`\>(`key`, `ttlMs`, `requestMethod`): `undefined` \| `Promise`\<`T`\>

• **key**: `string`

The key for the entry in the cache.

• **ttlMs**: `undefined` \| `number`

The TTL of the entry in the cache.
